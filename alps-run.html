Field notes

Weather

  • Start early — storms from ~2pm in July
  • Aim to clear summits by midday

Cash

  • All huts cash only — carry €100+
  • Only ATM: Passo San Pellegrino (Stage 3)

Kit

  • Linen sleeping bag liner — required everywhere
  • 1.5L water minimum between huts
  • All GPX downloaded to your watch

Emergency

  • General: 112
  • Mountain rescue: 118
  • Download offline maps — no signal up high
